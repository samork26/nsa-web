How can I become a member?

  • Membership Dues are $35 for the academic year (Fall '23-Spring '24) or are $20 for an individual semester.
  • Membership payments can be made through Venmo, @neuroscienceallianceucf.
  • Please be sure to indicate your Name and T-shirt size in the payment description.
  • We also accept cash directed to our Executive Board.
    •

    How can I reach Active Member Status?

    Active Member Criteria include being a Dues Paying Member, attending 2+ GBMs of the current semester, and completing 5+ volunteer hours. The perks of being an active member include access to special events, graduation cords, and discounted merchandise!
